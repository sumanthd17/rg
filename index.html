WHY is a weekly reading group session where members from AI4Bharat gather for a 1hr presentation on recent advances in Speech and Natural Language Processing.
See this semester's schedule here. Old talks (including some videos) are in our archive.

Details

WHY is held on Fridays from 11:00 am to 12:00 pm (unless otherwise noted) in AI4Bharat Lab (in the SSB building, IIT Madras). Everyone is welcome.
